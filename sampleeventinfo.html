Goat Yoga

Ideal for beginners and seasoned yogis!

Step away from the noise and unwind with a unique wellness experience on our peaceful little farm. Every Saturday at 10 AM, we host a guided yoga session where you’ll be joined by our friendly, curious goats who love to interact with guests throughout the class.

Whether they’re wandering around your mat, gently nudging you for attention, or simply adding a bit of playful charm to the practice, they create a relaxing and joyful atmosphere. It’s the perfect blend of mindfulness, movement, and a little farm style fun.

Turkey Fest

SECOND ANNUAL TURKEY LEG FEST!

Get ready, because the holiday season is about to go wild! The SECOND ANNUAL TURKEY LEG FEST is back and bigger than ever!!! If you’re even slightly obsessed with turkey legs, this is your miracle come true.

We’re talking 10 food trucks, each serving insane, over-the-top turkey leg creations you won’t find anywhere else! Smoked, glazed, stuffed, dipped, dripping, spicy, sweet… you name it, someone’s serving it. Pair it with our legendary seasonal eggnog and you’re officially in holiday heaven.
The vibes? WILD LIKE A TURKEY. We’ve got Christmas movies blasting on a giant projector, music pumping, lights glowing, a pie eating contest and people dancing off their food comas. This isn’t just a festival, it’s THE holiday kickoff. If you’re not here… well then you’re missing out!

Jester Market

Are you up to no good?

Do you enjoy stirring up a little harmless trouble? Does the pull of secrets and sly smiles tempt you? Then step into the Jester Market, a place where mischief is encouraged and curiosity is always rewarded.

Imagine a refined version of Mardi Gras with lanterns glowing overhead, masked performers drifting between the crowds, and laughter weaving through the night like a quiet spell. Entertainment fills every corner. Jesters tell clever jokes, storytellers captivate with their tales, and performers seem to know exactly when you need a laugh.

The vendors are part of the fun too. Each booth feels like a world of playful trickery with strange curios, unusual collectibles, and rare treasures offered with a wink or a riddle. Browsing often becomes a game and discovering something special feels like solving a secret.

If you are drawn to the unusual, the humorous, and the wonderfully bizarre, the Jester Market is your sanctuary. Step inside and let the night pull you deeper. The jokes, the mysteries, and the mischief are waiting.
